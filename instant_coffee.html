Instant coffee


Description


Instant coffee is a type of coffee dissolved in
hot water/cold water/hot milk/cold milk instantly to make a drink.


Ingredients


  • Hot water and or milk
  • Instant coffee powder/granules
  • sugar or honey

Steps

  1. Fetch cooking pot for holding the water.
  2. PLace cooking pot under tap.
  3. Gently open tap to let water into the pot.
  4. Close tap once cooking pot is three quarters full.
  5. PLace cooking pot on stove and turn the stove on to medium heat.
  6. Wait for the water to boil for at least one minute.
  7. Once water has boiled, serve it in a cup.
  8. Add coffee and sugar or honey to taste.
